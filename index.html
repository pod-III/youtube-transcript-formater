YouTube Transcript Formatter


      

    


    

      

        
Welcome

        
This page will help you edit a YouTube video transcript

        
How to Use:

        
    
          
  1. Open any video you want to transcript
    2. 
          
  2. 
            Go to the video description and click the "more"
            
    
              
            
    
          
    3. 
          
  3. 
            Click the "Show Transcript" and it'll show the transcript box on the
            left side of the video
            
    
              
            
    
          
    4. 
          
  4. 
            Toggle the timestamp
            
    
              
            
    
          
    5. 
          
  5. 
            Select the desired transcript and copy it
            
    
              
            
    
          
    6. 
          
  6. Paste your YouTube transcript in the input box.
    7. 
          
  7. Click "Edit" to process the transcript.
    8. 
          
  8. 
            Click "Separate" to separate the processed transcript:
            
      
              
    • 
                Separate button separates the text based on few key words or
                phrase that might signified that a different speaker is speaking
                (e.g. "three, two, one" or "prime minister")
              
      • 
              
    • These phrases is hard coded, and if you have suggestion, please post an issue on GitHub
      • 
            
    
          
    9. 
          
  9. Use "Copy" to copy the formatted transcript.
    10. 
          
  10. Click "Save" to download the transcript as a text file.
