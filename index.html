YouTube Transcript Formatter

Welcome

This page will help you edit a YouTube video transcript

How to Use:

  1. Open any video you want to transcript

  2. Go to the video description and click the "more"

  3. Click the "Show Transcript" and it'll show the transcript box on the left side of the video

  4. Toggle the timestamp

  5. Select the desired transcript and copy it

  6. Paste your YouTube transcript in the input box.
  7. Click "Edit" to process the transcript.
  8. Click "Separate" to separate the processed transcript
  9. Use "Copy" to copy the formatted transcript.
  10. Click "Save" to download the transcript as a text file.
